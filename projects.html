One Nation - One Funding

Problem Statement: To upgrade the Higher education system in India, several agencies give funding under various projects/schemes. Many HEIs are applying for multiple funding schemes under various categories. Hence, there is a need for an integrated portal where all agencies will upload the details of the scheme and eligibility for applying. Also, the portal shall help with the distribution of schemes to HEIs. This system shall provide all funding-specific data to the stakeholders with proper authentication about the funding schemes. The major focus of this problem statement is to 1. Create a gateway for various funding agencies to submit their problem statements and funding limit 2. Every HEIs or individual will register into the portal and upload their proposal 3. An online expert evaluation panel will be there for interviewing the applicants, project presentation, finalization of results, and sanctioning letters to promote the One Nation – One funding platform for the funding agencies and applicants.

Built a website to make funding agencies and educational institutions get connected.

Implemented using React.js, Node.js & MySQL.
